Cheesecake Labs ranked #2 amongst Top Developers in Latin America by Clutch

with Alex CordeiroPublicado em novembro 15, 2017

Cheesecake Labs is very excited to be featured in Clutch’s latest announcement of its Top Latin American Developers & Agencies 2017, which recognizes the leading regional technology development, marketing, design, and related digital services companies with excellent client satisfaction.

We are very proud of our team and our work for this important recognition from this leading B2B tech services research and reviews platforms. Our company creates a new way to collaboratively build amazing products and full-stack web and mobile app solutions. We believe in being highly available, working with passion, planning for the long-run, and fostering relationships that benefit all parts. Our Cakers are always up-to-date with the latest technologies and processes, and they continuously seek for improvement.

This is all reflected in our great ratings and reviews featured on Cheesecake Labs profile on Clutch with client reviews. The B2B research platform’s ratings and reviews include a combination of metrics, ranging from experience in the sector and market presence to types of clients and ability to deliver awesome results delivered to clients. Furthermore, Clutch’s dedicated analysts interviewed our current and past clients to accurately portray our strengths on our profile.

We are excited to celebrate these accomplishments and our great clients who showed their appreciation for our work. These honors would not have been possible without the time they took to provide their detailed client reviews.

 

Here are some of our customer’s amazing comments:

“We liked Cheesecake because they’re nearshore—only four hours ahead—so we can have meetings with them whenever we want… they’re cooler and younger, so they fit the demographic we’re going after and they use really modern tools.” — CEO, Music Platform

“There is no gap in communication, which is unique for offshore development, in my opinion. Cheesecake Labs is a highly-intuitive group which comes to every single decision with multiple scenarios of how they would like to potentially solve them intuitively, and based off of best practices.” – Co-Founder, Nutrition Tech Startup

“The biggest reason we went with Cheesecake is because they had already worked on a number of other impressive Silicon Valley apps, and contributed to the success of those companies … They did a fantastic job helping us manage the project and making sure that we hit the goals.” – CEO, IoT Device Company

“I am very happy with the work that Cheesecake Labs did for us. In addition to recommending them, I have subsequently used Cheesecake on another smaller personal project and would happily do so again.” – Founder, Media Search Engine

We are both humbled and excited for these kind reviews and to have been featured among other top regional firms. We are also grateful to our talented Cakers’ team, which continues developing high-quality mobile and web technologies with meaning. As Cheesecake Labs continues to strive and we will keep up with our commitment to our clients.

 

Clutch’s full research can be found at: Top App Developers in Latin America
